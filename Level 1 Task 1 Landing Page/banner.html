About Us

Pureco mineral water emerges from a place in the heart of natural resources, purity & bottling at an ecological plant & is ensured perfectly at every level.

Pureco premium water is an invention of Mr. Sumeet Prachande. Pureco is premium quality water & a product by Krushna Jal. Pureco ensures that it is manufactured in only state of art plants which are certified by BIS & ISO 9001 2008 (Food Safety).

Our every bottle contains water treated through our step state of art process in comprise sand filtration, carbon filtration, micron filter, reverse osmosis, polish filter, minerals addition, UV treatment & ozonisation.
